# Apollo Asset Backed Credit Company (ABC)

## Product Description

Apollo Asset Backed Credit Company (ABC) is described as "a semi liquid, turnkey solution that can provide investors access to high-quality, asset backed instruments across a diverse range of sectors."

The investment vehicle aims to deliver superior yields compared to publicly traded credit instruments of similar quality.

## Investment Strategy & Focus

**Asset Classes & Sectors:**
ABC provides exposure across diversified asset-backed instruments. The company sources investments through multiple channels:
- Direct origination via proprietary platforms
- Strategic partnerships
- Primary market participation
- Secondary market opportunities

**Platform Strength:**
The company leverages Apollo's broader Asset-Backed Finance (ABF) platform, which maintains established channels for sourcing opportunities across various credit sectors.

## Management & Governance

The company is led by experienced professionals in structured credit and asset-backed finance:

- **Michael Paniwozik** — President and partner specializing in structured credit investing
- **Stuart Rothstein** — Board Chair and COO of Real Assets and Asset-Backed Finance

## Target Investor Base

ABC targets wealth professionals, financial advisors, and institutional investors seeking alternatives to traditional credit investments with enhanced yield potential.

## Context: Apollo's ABF Market Position

- Apollo's Credit business spans private and public corporate credit and asset-backed finance markets
- Global expansion in 2024: deepening UK/EU middle-market and ABF presence, building distribution in Japan and Korea
- Capturing bank retrenchment opportunities and rising demand for private credit globally
