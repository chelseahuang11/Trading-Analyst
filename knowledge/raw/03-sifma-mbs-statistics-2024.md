# US Mortgage-Backed Securities Statistics — SIFMA

## Overview

SIFMA Research provides comprehensive tracking of the U.S. mortgage-backed securities market, encompassing issuance, trading, and outstanding data across both agency and non-agency securities.

## YTD 2026 Statistics (Through March)

**Issuance Performance:**
- Total MBS issuance reached $540.8 billion, +30.0% Y/Y

**Agency Trading Volumes:**
- Average daily volume of $412.8 billion ADV, +13.6% Y/Y

**Non-Agency Trading Volumes:**
- Average daily volume of $2,142.1 million ADV, +28.0% Y/Y

## 2024 Full-Year Highlights

- MBS issuance increased 21.6% year-over-year to $1.6 trillion in 2024

## Data Coverage

The statistics track several key metrics:
- Issuance data broken down by agency and non-agency securities
- Trading volumes reported in number of trades and dollar amounts
- Outstanding balances across MBS subcategories
- Trend analysis

## Available Data Formats

Data is accessible through multiple timeframes including daily (trading volumes only), monthly, quarterly, and annual statistics.
