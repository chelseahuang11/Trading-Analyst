# SIFMA Research Quarterly: Fixed Income Issuance and Trading — 1Q26

## Overview

SIFMA's Research Quarterly report for 1Q26 provides comprehensive data on U.S. fixed income markets, encompassing Treasury securities, mortgage-backed securities, corporate bonds, municipal securities, agency securities, and asset-backed securities.

## Key Issuance Data

### Total Fixed Income Issuance
Fixed income issuance posted another strong quarter at $3.2 trillion (+13.7% Q/Q, +10.3% Y/Y). This marked the first quarter exceeding $3.0 trillion since 4Q21, with five of six asset classes experiencing quarterly growth.

### Treasury Issuance
Long-term UST issuance remained robust at $1.2 trillion, representing +2.5% quarterly and +2.3% year-over-year increase. This marks the seventh consecutive quarter with issuance above $1 trillion.

### Corporate Bond Issuance
Corporate issuance reached $775.2 billion with +70.3% Q/Q, +15.6% Y/Y gains — the largest quarterly total since 2Q20.

### Mortgage-Backed Securities (MBS)
MBS posted a strong quarter with issuance of $540.8 billion (+1.0% Q/Q, +30.0% Y/Y), indicating sustained housing finance activity.

### Municipal Bonds
Municipal bond issuance totaled $127.8 billion, representing the only asset class with a quarterly decline at -12.3% Q/Q, though maintaining +5.8% Y/Y growth.

## Trading Volume Data

### Average Daily Trading Volumes
Aggregate trading volumes averaged $1.8 trillion, up +24.9% Q/Q and +20.2% Y/Y.

### Asset-Specific Average Daily Trading Volumes
- **UST**: $1,288.8 billion (+28.0% Q/Q)
- **MBS**: $414.9 billion (+16.4% Q/Q)
- **Corporates**: $71.4 billion (+29.5% Q/Q)
- **Agency Securities**: $3.4 billion (+1.6% Q/Q)
- **ABS**: $2.7 billion (+50.5% Q/Q)

All categories experienced quarterly increases following a slower 4Q25 performance.

## Federal Reserve Action

At the March FOMC meeting, the Fed maintained the target range for the Fed Funds rate at 3.50%–3.75%, with almost all members supporting the decision.

## Full-Year 2026 YTD (Through March)
- Total fixed income issuance: $3,208.8 billion, +10.3% Y/Y
- Total trading: $1,794.9 billion, +20.2% Y/Y
- Outstanding (excluding MBS and ABS, as of 4Q25): $49.6 trillion, +5.8% Y/Y
