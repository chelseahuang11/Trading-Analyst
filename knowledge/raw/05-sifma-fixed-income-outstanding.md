# US Fixed Income Securities Statistics — SIFMA

## Overview

SIFMA Research monitors U.S. fixed income markets, tracking data across multiple asset classes and market activities. The organization provides downloadable statistics on a monthly, quarterly, and annual basis.

## YTD 2026 Key Metrics (As of April 2, 2026)

- **Issuance (through March):** $3,208.8 billion, +10.3% Y/Y
- **Trading (through March):** $1,794.9 billion, +20.2% Y/Y
- **Outstanding (excluding MBS and ABS, as of 4Q25):** $49.6 trillion, +5.8% Y/Y

## Market Coverage

SIFMA's research encompasses several fixed income sectors:

- U.S. Treasuries
- Mortgage-backed securities (MBS)
- Corporate bonds
- Municipal securities
- Federal agency securities
- Asset-backed securities (ABS)
- Money markets (outstanding data only)

## Available Resources

The organization provides multiple data tools and reports:

- Fixed Income Data Visualization (interactive tool)
- Research Quarterly reports on issuance, trading, and outstanding securities
- Downloadable Excel files with detailed breakdowns
- Trend analysis across all tracked categories

Data subscribers can receive email alerts when new statistics are published.

## MBS Market Context

The MBS market remains one of the largest segments of the U.S. fixed income universe, with total outstanding balances tracking alongside the $49.6 trillion aggregate fixed income market (ex-MBS/ABS). Agency MBS continues to dominate issuance, while non-agency/private-label RMBS represents a growing share of new supply in the post-pandemic era.
